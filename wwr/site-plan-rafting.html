Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this white water rafting website is to serve older adults with a dedicated online resource and community hub that caters to the unique interests and needs of older individuals seeking the enjoyment of safe white water rafting experiences. We aim to inspire, educate, and empower older adults to embrace the excitement of white water rafting as a means to stay active, connect with like-minded peers, and embark on memorable outdoor adventures that contribute to their overall well-being and sense of fulfillment.

Audience

The audience for a white water rafting website targeting older adults typically includes individuals aged 50 and above who have an interest in outdoor adventure activities and are looking for white water rafting experiences that are well-suited to their age and physical capabilities.
