Overview

Purpose

I plan on building a website that visualizes my professional background by highlighting the different areas of expertise, professional experience, and education & certifications, I've gained over the last 30 years. The primary audience will be prospective clients/employers and help to create a portfolio of businesses and clients I've served. I anticipate this will require a main page and 2 supporting pages. I'll draw on prior material I've created to provide the core content and try to visualize it in a concise and impactful manner.

Audience

My audience is prospective employers or clients that might be interested in learning more about who I am and what are competencies.

Content

Home page

I am a proven financial leader with over 30 years of experience in leadership positions, holding a CPA certification and an MBA in Finance and Accounting and other certifications that demonstrate my commitment to continuous learning and improvement. I have a proven track record of delivering excellence through strategic planning, risk management, and data-driven decision-making. My expertise in finance, accounting, and program management, coupled with my ability to lead multi-disciplinary teams, has consistently delivered actionable insights and improved organizational performance. I am committed to fostering a collaborative and agile team environment. My goal is to contribute to the organization's success by optimizing operational effectiveness, reducing risk, and driving sustainable growth.

I've served all levels of management from Regional VPs, CEOs, CFOs, as well as clients from various industries, large and small. I've also owned my own business and understand the risks and concerns of key stakeholders. My focus is on delivering results that drive sustainable value for the business and all stakeholders.
